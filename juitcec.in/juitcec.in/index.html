About CEC

CIVIL ENGINEERING CONSORTIUM

Civil engineering consortium of JUIT is an active student organization that was first formed in February, 2008. CEC, as is known in short, has evolved with the changing winds of time. However the essence of forming this body remains the same- to bring forth the interaction between civil engineering students so that ideas are shared and knowledge ingested. At CEC, we work in concordance to organize technical conferences, workshops and seminars to spread awareness amongst students and teachers updating everyone's technical know-how. On these occasions, guest lecturers from around the country come to share their experience and knowledge. Other than these educative proceedings, fun-events like competitions on bridge building etc are also catalogued because we believe, good dosages of FUN is a must for living well. Sanrachna is the annual produce of the work done by CEC. Literary and technical teams work to meticulously entwine engineering argots with linguistic skills. And other clubs like the cultural club, event club, photography club etc toil to give the necessary dash of shine, shimmer and sparkle.
